Mission of Early Saints
See also Israel, Mission of
in thee shall all families of the earth be blessed, Gen. 12:3 (Gal. 3:8; Abr. 2:11).
in thy seed shall all the nations of the earth be blessed, Gen. 22:18 (D&C 110:12).
Ye are the salt of the earth, Matt. 5:13 (3 Ne. 12:13).
Ye are the light of the world, Matt. 5:14.
Be ye therefore perfect, Matt. 5:48 (3 Ne. 12:48).
seek ye first the kingdom of God, Matt. 6:33 (3 Ne. 13:33).
Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing, Matt. 28:19.
repentance ... should be preached in his name among all nations, Luke 24:47.
go and bring forth fruit, John 15:16.
Ye are the children ... of the covenant which God made with our fathers, Acts 3:25 (3 Ne. 20:25).
called to be saints, 1 Cor. 1:2.
we ... as Isaac was, are the children of promise, Gal. 4:28.
put on the new man, Eph. 4:24.
perverse nation, among whom ye shine as lights, Philip. 2:15.
royal priesthood, an holy nation, a peculiar people, 1 Pet. 2:9.
persuade our children, and also our brethren, to believe in Christ, 2 Ne. 25:23.
teach them the word of God, Jacob 1:19 (Alma 38:10).
Preach unto them repentance, and faith, Alma 37:33.
Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel, Morm. 9:22.
See also Ps. 145:10; Eccl. 12:13; Ezek. 33:1-11; D&C 86:2.
