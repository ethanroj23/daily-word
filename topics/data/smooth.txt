Smooth, Smoother
Esau ... is a hairy man, and I am a smooth man, Gen. 27:11.
he ... chose him five smooth stones, 1 Sam. 17:40.
words of his mouth were smoother than butter, Ps. 55:21.
her mouth is smoother than oil, Prov. 5:3.
speak unto us smooth things, Isa. 30:10.
rough ways shall be made smooth, Luke 3:5 (1 Ne. 17:46; D&C 49:23; 109:74).
if ye shall say unto this mountain ... become smooth, Hel. 10:9.
many smooth places became rough, 3 Ne. 8:13 (Hel. 12:10).
