Abound
because iniquity shall abound, the love of many shall wax cold, Matt. 24:12 (D&C 45:27; JS—M 1:30).
where sin abounded, grace did much more abound, Rom. 5:20.
Shall we continue in sin, that grace may abound, Rom. 6:1.
peace in believing, that ye may abound in hope, Rom. 15:13.
always abounding in the work of the Lord, 1 Cor. 15:58.
sufferings of Christ abound in us, so our consolation also aboundeth, 2 Cor. 1:5.
as ye abound in every thing, … see that ye abound in this grace, 2 Cor. 8:7.
God is able to make all grace abound toward you, 2 Cor. 9:8.
abounded toward us in all wisdom and prudence, Eph. 1:8.
in the faith, … abounding therein with thanksgiving, Col. 2:7.
abound in love one toward another, 1 Thes. 3:12.
if these things be in you, and abound, 2 Pet. 1:8 (D&C 107:31).
always abound in good works, Alma 7:24 (Mosiah 5:15; Ether 12:4).
